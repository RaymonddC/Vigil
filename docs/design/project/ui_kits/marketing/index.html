01 · Never acts alone

Human in the loop by design.

Every alert is drafted by Vigil and approved by a named clinician before it's written to the EHR. The audit trail attributes every action to a person.

02 · Information-dense

Eye lands on the one patient that matters.

A roster sorted by risk, a five-level scale readable in under a second, and tabular vitals everywhere. Designed for a 12-hour shift, not a product demo.

03 · Post-op and postpartum

One pipeline, two wards.

The same deterministic 7-state agent monitors surgical recovery and postpartum care. No different flows, no different branding — clinicians move between wards without relearning the tool.

The SBAR card is the artifact.

Vigil's agent watches FHIR vitals, runs NEWS2 and qSOFA, evaluates trends, and when a pattern emerges it drafts a Situation / Background / Assessment / Recommendation note — the format every clinician already trusts.

  • Every claim is cited to specific vitals and lab values
  • Clinician edits inline before approving
  • Approved notes land in the EHR attributed to the approver
  • Dismissed drafts are retained for audit, not silently discarded
